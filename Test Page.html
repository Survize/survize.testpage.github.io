What is meme?

A meme is a cultural idea, behavior, or style that spreads from person to person within a society. It can be a piece of content, often humorous, that spreads quickly through social media, websites, or other digital platforms. Memes usually consist of images, videos, or text, and they often carry humorous or satirical messages, referencing popular culture, current events, or shared experiences.


Memes are characterized by their ability to evolve as people modify or remix them, leading to various versions and interpretations. The format can be reused and adapted, making memes a versatile way to convey humor or social commentary.In modern usage, memes are mostly associated with internet culture, but they can be found in many forms, from the classic "image macros" (pictures with text overlaid) to viral video clips and trends.
